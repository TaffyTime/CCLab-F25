bio-astronomers and astro-biologists at the Inter-Galactic Communication Hub

Family:

Photo-Fungus is a timid species that tends to roam isolated though occasionally run into one another. These encounters either result in deep bonds and life partnership or in death by social anxiety

Grassy Space Fields in the far edges of quiet galaxies

A layered spherical creature with sun ray arms. Multicolored and color changing

Key Characteristics:

  • Breathing
